World Cup Records

There are many records being held by players at this moment for the world cup. I have chosen three records the cards below will include the following information; who has the most goals in the world cup, who has the most assists in the world cup, and who won the most world cups ever individually.


Who has the most goals in the World Cup?


Miroslav Josef Klose is the head coach of the Austrian Bundesliga club Rheindorf Altach and a former footballer from Germany. Klose, a striker, is Germany's all-time leading scorer and holds the record for the most goals scored in FIFA World Cups (16) spanning four tournaments.

Who has the most assists at the World Cup?


Lionel Andrés Messi, also known as Leo Messi, is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and captains the Argentina national team. Lionel Messi became the player with the most assists on record in World Cup knockout matches on Friday, surpassing Pele when he teed up Nahuel Molina's goal against the Netherlands.

Who has won the most World Cup trophies?


Edson Arantes do Nascimento, known as Pelé, is a Brazilian former professional footballer who played as a forward. Brazil's Pelé is the only one to have won three times, while another 20 have won twice. No player has won two World Cups both as captain.
